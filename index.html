My College Journey & Goals

Hi, I’m Zahra Batool, a Communication Design major at City Tech. I transferred from QCC after finishing my associate’s and am now working toward the BFA, so I’m basically a junior. My first semester here was rough, but this one is off to a better start. I’m on campus three days a week; Tuesdays are the longest (12–9 p.m.), and the two-hour commute each way gets tough sometimes, but I feel steadier overall. Looking ahead, I want to blend my passion for darkroom photography and videography with my path as a graphic designer and motion-graphics animator. I plan to pursue internships in these areas to see how real studios operate, develop my own style, and ultimately grow into an art director.
